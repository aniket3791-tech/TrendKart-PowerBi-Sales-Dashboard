# Business Insights

## Sales Trends

- Sales reached their highest levels during October and November.
- Sales in 2025 exceeded those in 2024.

## Product Performance

- Electronics generated the highest revenue.
- Fashion products recorded the highest sales volume.

## Regional Analysis

- South region generated the highest revenue.
- Maharashtra was the top-performing state.

## Customer Analysis

- Average Order Value was approximately ₹5.2K.
- Around 600 unique customers were identified.

## Returns

- Overall return rate was 5.84%.
- North region recorded the highest return rate.

## Recommendations

- Increase promotions during high-performing months.
- Expand successful product categories.
- Improve return management in high-return regions.
- Introduce customer loyalty and referral programs.
