# DAX Measures

## Total Sales

Calculates the total revenue generated.

---

## Total Orders

Counts all completed orders.

---

## Average Order Value (AOV)

Average revenue generated per order.

---

## Distinct Customers

Counts unique customers.

---

## Returned Orders

Counts returned orders.

---

## Return Rate

Percentage of returned orders.

---

## Total Quantity Sold

Calculates total quantity sold.

---

## Sales YTD

Year-to-date sales.

---

## Previous Year Sales

Sales generated in the previous year.

---

## Top 10 Buyers

Ranks customers based on sales.
