# Business Objectives

## Problem Statement

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional data every day. Without proper reporting, it is difficult to monitor sales performance, customer behaviour, and product trends.

This Power BI dashboard was developed to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights.

## Objectives

- Monitor total sales performance
- Analyze monthly sales trends
- Identify top-performing products
- Track regional sales performance
- Evaluate customer purchasing behaviour
- Monitor return rates
- Support executive decision-making through interactive dashboards

## Target Users

- Business Managers
- Sales Executives
- Regional Managers
- Marketing Team
